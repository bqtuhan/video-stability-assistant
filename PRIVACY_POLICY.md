# Privacy Policy for Video Stability Assistant

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

Video Stability Assistant ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information We Collect (Or Rather, Don't Collect)

Video Stability Assistant is designed with privacy as a foundational principle. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or video content to external servers.**

All video playback metrics, stability scoring, and freeze predictions are computed locally within your browser.

## 2. Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.sync` or `chrome.storage.local`) to save your preferences, such as:
* Playback mode selection (Balanced, Live, VOD)
* Notification preferences
* Sampling intervals
* Theme selection
* Site allowlists

This data remains on your device and, if applicable, syncs across your signed-in browser profiles according to your browser's built-in synchronization features. We do not have access to this data.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Video Stability Assistant does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising scripts. The extension operates entirely offline and does not make external network requests.

## 4. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Because we do not collect, process, or store any personal data, Video Stability Assistant is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

You have complete control over your data:
* **Right to Access & Delete:** You can view and delete all saved preferences at any time by clicking the "Reset to Defaults" button in the extension's Settings page, or by uninstalling the extension.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/bqtuhan/video-stability-assistant/issues).
